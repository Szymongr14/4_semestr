Peruvian President Alberto Fujimori has issued an order confining all military and police to their barracks .
The President is locked in a power struggle with former intelligence service chief Vladimiro Monticenos .
Mr. Fujimori told reporters late Wednesday , he ordered a complete freeze on movements by the Armed Forces .
President Fujimori has been leading a purge of the military of those loyal to Mr. Monticenos , the former spy master , whose surprise return to Peru on Monday triggered speculation of a possible coup .
